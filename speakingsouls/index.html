Speaking Souls

Speaking Souls is a multilingual Spoken Word and Open Mic event that is held in many cities across India. The Speaking Souls stage aims to provide a platform for writers, poets, storytellers, comedians, musicians, and talented individual to express their skills in front of a non â€“ judgmental, encouraging and intimate audience. Started in December 2017, it is one of the fastest growing literary and creative event chains in India with more than 1000 performers and audience being a part of its ever-growing family.

Speaking Souls has arrived to the City of Gold! Out first International event kicks off at Urban Bistro, Al Sofouh, Dubai on March 1st and be a part of the magical evening while jamming with the most creative people in the city!

What can you perform?

Literature

Spill out your stories, poems, letters, tales or just anything that you have written or scribbled. At Speaking Souls, each word is felt in the heart and appreciated deeply. Some are raw, some are impactful but all are beautiful.

Comedy

Whether you are a budding comedian or an experienced one, whether you are a performer or want to test your set, the stage of Speaking Souls encourages you to boost your presence in the comedy scene.

Music

Both Indie musicians and bands often find their way to the Speaking Souls stage to perform in front of an encouraging audience. The talent of the music you bring just adds to the magic of the event.

Talent

Do you beat-box? Are you a magician? Can you rap? Speaking Souls have been the platform for some insane talents across cities and even you could be one in the next event! Do not shy from expressing it on our stage.
